Rwanda Booking Tour are the industry leaders in the tours and travel trade and represent a wide spectrum of travel and vacation packages available in Rwanda


Booking Permits & Services

Tourism services can be booked via our online services, irembo This includes gorilla and primate tracking permits, trekking, city tours and nature walks. A full price list is available on the platform


Airlines & Visas

All international flights arrive at Kigali International Airport, just 10km from Kigali city centre. Nationals of all countries receive a visa on arrival at Kigali International Airport and all land borders. Airlines servicing Kigali International Airport are: RwandAir, Kenya Airways, Ethiopian Airlines, Brussels Airlines, KLM, Qatar Airways and Turkish Airlines. Helicopter transfers within Rwanda are available through Akagera Aviation


Our Vision

A world where everyone, regardless of ability, can experience the beauty and power of nature. Our vision is to support community to conserve natural resources and maximize the tourism benefits by increasing the number of tourists in Rwanda towards sustainable development.
